My Projects

This page about my Pong Project. My partner and I worked on this project every chance we got, we were constantly trying out new things in order to make our project automatic. Although, at the end of it all we were not successful in that category, we did accomplish the main idea of this project; we got a ping pong ball to go over a ping pong net by using Arduino. We created a small cattapult built with wooden thick popcicle sticks, rubber bands, and hot glue. We used arduino by having a motor hold the cattapult in place until we decided to move it through the red board. Overall enjoyed creating this project and I also got to learn along the way :))
